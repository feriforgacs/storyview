AMP Story support in the Story View WordPress plugin

With the help of Story View, you can easily create AMP Stories. All you need to do is enable the AMP Story version of your story on your WordPress admin and upload, insert the required information.

After you provided the necessary information, your AMP Story will be available and visible on a separate URL. You can find this URL on your admin panel (AMP Story Preview URL) or you can visit it by clicking on the embedded Story View button and open it on a new page.

Here is a short video about how to set up an AMP Story

Why does it work like that?

With this solution, normal visitors of your website will see the classic version of your story, while the link to the AMP Story version will also be available to the search bots (like the Google bot) to find and add it to their index. This way you can provide a great visual experience to your visitors while keeping them on your website, also your stories will be available on the search result page to help you grow your audience.

Frequently asked questions about AMP Story support in Story View

  • Can I create my AMP Story with totally different content and layout than my Story View?
    At the moment, this is not possible. The AMP version of your story will be generated from the blocks you added to your Story View.
  • Why do I need to add another block to my stories when I enable AMP Story?
    For AMP Story a cover block is mandatory and in most cases, this cover is not the same as the first block is your Story View.
  • Can I create an AMP Story without creating a Story View?
    No, and yes :) You should create your story the same way you would do with a normal Story View, but you have the option not to add the shortcode ([ff_storyview]) to your post. This way, the classic Story View won't be visible for your visitors, but the AMP Story becomes available.
    To make sure that search engines will find the AMP Story, you should make the URL of it publicly available. The easiest way to do that is by adding a link at the end of your post.
  • How is this going to help me grow my audience?
    By using AMP Story, the chance that someone will find you through Google search is much better. At the end of each AMP Story, there is a link to the full article, this way a user who visited your AMP Story could easily visit your website as well.
  • Can I change the animations in the AMP Story?
    At the moment this is not supported by the plugin.
  • Can I change the sharing options in the AMP Story?
    At the moment this is not supported by the plugin.
  • Can I add custom blocks (embed HTML or shortcodes) to AMP Story?
    At the moment this is not supported by the plugin.
  • Can I add Analytics tracking to AMP Story?
    Yes you can. Go to the setting of the Story View plugin, click on the AMP Settings tab and add your tracking id to the Google Analytics ID field.
