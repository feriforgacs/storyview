Install Story View on your WordPress site

Installing Story View on your WordPress site doesn't take more than a minute.

Log in to your WordPress admin, go to your plugins and select the option to add a new plugin. Upload the Story View plugin package, start the installation process then turn the plugin on.

If everything worked fine, You'll see a new item in the left side navigation of your WordPress admin and if you start to edit one of your posts, at the bottom of it, you'll find a new option to turn Story View on for that post. Feel free to turn it on, it won't change anything in your posts.

Take a look at the following video to view the process.

Now that you installed the plugin, you are ready to create your first story.
