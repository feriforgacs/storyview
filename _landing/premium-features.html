Premium features

The following features are only available in the Pro version of the plugin:

Custom Blocks

Custom Blocks makes it possible to add extra functionality to your stories. With the help of it you can add contact forms, subscription forms, YouTube videos and almost any kinds of custom HTML to your stories. This feature can also help you monetize your stories because with custom HTML you can display ads in your stories.
Take a look at the list below to learn more about this feature.

  • Add a Contact form to your story with the help of Custom Story blocks
  • Add a MailChimp subscription form to your story with the help of Custom Story blocks
  • Embed a YouTube video to your story with the help of Custom Story blocks

Button Designer

In some cases, the design of the built-in story buttons is just not right for the theme of your WordPress site. The Button Designer feature can help you resolve this problem.

AMP Story Support

With the help of the built-in AMP Story support, you can boost the appearance of your website in Google search.
All you need to do is enable the AMP version of your story, and upload some basic information.

Social share and recommended article

Enabling your visitors to share your content on social platforms is a key factor to drive more traffic to your website. That's why you can enable this on the end screen of your story.
If you'd like to keep your visitors on your website, the recommended article at the end of your story can help you do that.
Click here to learn how.

Social Share for all story blocks

If you want to generate more social share for your stories, you can turn it on not just for the end screen but for all your story blocks.
